Biography

I am currently a student at Northeastern University studying for a Master's degree in computer science. Although I was raised in Santa Cruz, California, I graduated from Brandeis University with a Bachelor's degree in Math and Studio Art. My passion for creative problem-solving led me to a career in finance, but after a few years, I realized I needed work that was more meaningful-for myself and my clients.

A foundational knowledge of design and logic led me to study programming and web design at UMASS Lowell, where I earned a Bachelor's in Information Technology with a Certificate in Web Design.
